Hero

The hero section is the first thing people see when they visit a website. It usually contains a large, eye-catching image or video, along with a brief message that introduces the website's purpose or main content. Its aim is to capture visitors' attention and encourage them to explore further. Think of it as the welcoming entrance to a digital space, setting the tone for the rest of the website.
