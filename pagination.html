Pagination and Carousel

Pagination

Pagination is like a book with many pages; it breaks up content into smaller sections so you can flip through them one at a time. It's a way to organize and navigate through large amounts of information, making it easier for you to find what you're looking for without overwhelming you with too much at once. Pagination often includes buttons like "Previous" and "Next" to help you move forward and backward through the pages.

Carousel

A carousel is like a rotating display for pictures or information on a website. It shows several items one after the other, often with buttons or arrows to move between them. Carousels make it easy to see different things without taking up too much space on the page.
