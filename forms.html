Forms

The forms section on a website is where users can input and submit information. It typically includes fields for users to enter data, such as their name, email address, and message. Forms can be used for various purposes, such as contact forms, sign-up forms, or feedback forms. They provide a structured way for users to interact with the website and submit information to the site owner or administrator.

Code


            <form method="post" action="coming-soon.html" class="c-gift-form">
            <div class="c-gift-form-group">
            <label for="name">Name</label>
            <input type="text" id="name" name="name" placeholder="Enter your name" required>
            </div>
            <div class="c-gift-form-group">
            <label for="email">Email address</label>
            <input type="email" id="email" name="email" placeholder="Enter your email" required>
            </div>
            <div class="c-gift-form-group">
            <label for="amount">Gift Amount</label>
            <input type="text" id="amount" name="amount" placeholder="Enter your gift amount" required>
            </div>
            <div class="c-gift-form-group">
            <label for="message">Message</label>
            <textarea id="message" name="message" placeholder="Enter your message" rows="5" required></textarea>
            </div>
            <button type="submit" class="u-bg-accent-color u-bg-primary-as-text l-ml-1">Submit</button>
            </form>
