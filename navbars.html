Navbars

Navbars are essential UI components typically located at the top of a webpage or application interface. They provide navigation links to various sections or pages within the site, aiding users in moving around the platform efficiently. Additionally, navbars often include branding elements like logos and can incorporate interactive features such as search bars or dropdown menus.
