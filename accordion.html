Accordion

An accordion is a user interface element often used on websites to organize and display content in a collapsible manner. It consists of a series of sections or panels, with one panel visible at a time while the others are hidden. Users can click or tap on a panel's header to expand it and reveal its content, while collapsing previously expanded panels. Accordions are commonly used to conserve space and present information in a structured and easily navigable format.
