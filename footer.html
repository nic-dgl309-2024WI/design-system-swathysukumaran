Footer

The footer section is located at the bottom of a webpage. It typically contains important links, such as contact information, privacy policy, and terms of service. Footers often include navigation menus and social media links to help visitors find more content or connect with the website. It serves as a convenient way for users to access additional information or resources before leaving the site.
